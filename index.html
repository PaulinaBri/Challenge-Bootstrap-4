Fashion - size zero

Fortunately, the size zero fashion is passing by. The pursuit of an imaginary ideal often ended with anorexia or bulimia in models. Which led to creating a not real pattern of beauty among teenagers.

Fashion plus size

Plus size fashion is a step into the future, a step towards the acceptance of larger women. However, it can also be a dangerous step to lose health. Let's say it clearly! Obesity is not healthy and should not be promoted as something normal and very beautiful. Round and full shapes are good, stretch marks are okay, but excessive weight may not be okay for health.
